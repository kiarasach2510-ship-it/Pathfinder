# Pathfinder — Demo Video Script (~2.5–3 min)

## 1. Hook (0:00–0:15)
**[Show: landing page]**

> "Every student gets asked 'what do you want to do with your life?' — and for most of us, that question is terrifying, not exciting. Career tools make it worse: clinical forms, generic quizzes, a wall of job titles you don't understand. Pathfinder is different — it's a conversation, not a form."

## 2. What it is (0:15–0:30)
**[Stay on landing page, point out the tagline]**

> "Pathfinder is an AI career companion that gets to know you through small, gentle questions — not one big overwhelming form — and only suggests careers once it actually understands you: your interests, how you like to work, what income and lifestyle matter to you, and what's realistic in today's job market."

**[Click "Start your journey"]**

## 3. The conversation (0:30–1:10)
**[Show: chat view, intro message already visible]**

> "It opens by just talking to you, like a person would."

**[Type a real answer, e.g. "confused"]**

> "Notice it never asks 'what's your dream job' — it asks small, specific things: do you like working with people or alone, indoors or outdoors, what you actually enjoy in your free time. It always reacts to what you said before asking the next thing, so it feels like a conversation, not a survey."

**[Send 2-3 more quick real answers, let a couple of replies show on screen]**

> "Once it's built up a real picture of you — including practical things like how much time you're willing to spend training, the income you want, and your ideal work environment — it's ready to suggest something."

## 4. Career suggestions (1:10–1:40)
**[Either let it naturally suggest, or nudge with "please suggest some careers now"]**

> "And here's where it gets specific."

**[Point at a career suggestion card as it renders]**

> "Each suggestion is a full card: what the job actually is, *why* it fits what you just told it, the realistic salary range, and — this part matters — a concrete way to actually try it out this week. Not 'explore this further,' an actual course, project, or person to talk to."

**[Click the star icon on a card]**

> "One tap saves it to your Saved Paths."

## 5. Saved Paths (1:40–2:00)
**[Click "Paths" in the nav]**

> "Saved Paths is where your starred careers live — and each one comes with a deeper dive: what education you'd actually need, specific skills to build, real university programs, and extracurriculars you could start in high school."

## 6. My Progress (2:00–2:20)
**[Click "My Progress" in the nav]**

> "And this page isn't a stats dashboard — it's reflective. It looks back at your whole conversation and gives you an honest read on how you've been feeling about your future, the values and patterns it's noticed in what you said, every career path you've explored so far, and one small, low-pressure next step. No grades, no judgment — just a mirror."

## 7. Sign-in & close (2:20–2:45)
**[Optional: quickly show the sign-in modal — Google + email link]**

> "Everything saves to your account — sign in with Google or a passwordless email link, and your saved paths and progress are there whenever you come back."

**[Cut back to landing page tagline]**

> "Pathfinder isn't trying to solve your future in one sitting. It's just trying to make the first conversation about it feel okay. Thanks for watching."

---

## Quick recording notes
- Keep typed chat answers short and natural — don't overthink them on camera, that's the point of the product.
- If the AI takes 15-20+ seconds to generate a career suggestion, consider trimming that wait in editing (jump cut) rather than narrating dead air.
- Record at a resolution/zoom level where the career card text is clearly legible (browser zoom 100%, window reasonably wide).
- Total runtime target: under 3 minutes — judges skim.
